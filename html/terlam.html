terlam

I was asked to design and lead Terlam to resemble and remind one of a 1920's apothecary, as Terlam was startup to look at maximizing sales for essential oils and body care products start-up. After a few different directions, we decided on a modified typeface for the main type and incorporating symbols related to the brand.

Terlam is currently on hold due to funding delay, but a full e-commerce site designed was in the works but isn't live at this time.
