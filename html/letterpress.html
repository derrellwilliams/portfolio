letterpress

One of my first jobs in design was with Hemmert Studio in Madison, New Jersey. One their specialties is letterpress printing and I had the opportunity to learn under the creative director, Curtis Hemmert. Some of these projects were designed and printed while working there, and others are when they invited me back to let me use the press for a bit and still Curtis would have to be mentioned as helping me.

Some of the projects here were personal projects and some more client-based. I always enjoyed using and setting the wood type the most, which you can see in the thank you cards and "Ay Papi" print in the images.
