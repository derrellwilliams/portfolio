altos

Altos Research is a company who tries to make understanding the influxes of prices in real estate market understandable and manageable. The company had three main sources of business that we were asked to differentiate and make more obvious in a complete overhaul of their website.

Other than the logo we redid their whole branding. One of the main challenges we encountered was creating consistent graphs for their data sets they would give their customers. These graphs had to be able to handle a very large amount of data with a lot of variables, and at a small size. So we had to create something that would keep in line with their branding across all of these many variables. You can see these graph creation models here
